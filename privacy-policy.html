🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hermes Content Creator ("we", "our", or "app") handles information when you use our application to post content to Threads.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Content Data: Text posts that you generate and choose to publish to Threads via our app.
  • Basic Account Info: We access your Threads username and user ID solely to publish posts on your behalf.
  • Technical Data: Access tokens required to authenticate with the Threads API (stored locally on your device only).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Publish content (text posts) to your Threads account as requested by you
  • Verify your API credentials

4. Data Storage

  • We do not store your Threads password.
  • Access tokens are stored locally in your device's `.env` file.
  • We do not maintain a central database of user posts or credentials.
  • Generated content is saved locally in your project directory (e.g., `generated_content_*.json`).

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information or content to third parties, except as required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Meta Threads API. Your use of Threads is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke API access at any time via Meta Developer Portal
  • Request deletion of any data (though we store minimal to no user data)
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns (see below)

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to delete your data, please contact us at:

📧 megatemran@gmail.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
